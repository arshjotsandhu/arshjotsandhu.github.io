Brock Boeser Injured After Massive Hit From Clutterbuck

Boeser was injured in the final minute of regulation time Monday when he tried to finish a check on Islander Cal Clutterbuck and was launched backward against the frame of an open door at the Vancouver bench. He immediately reached for his lower back, then lay motionless on the ice while being attended by trainer Jon Sanderson. Boeser was helped off the ice, then taken to hospital after the Canucks won 4-3 in overtime.

Kobe Bryant Wins Oscar

Bryant, the retired Los Angeles Lakers star, brought home the golden statue for his contributions to the animated short "Dear Basketball," based on a poem he wrote in 2015 announcing his impending retirement from basketball. As executive producer, Bryant accepted his Oscar from "Star Wars'' star Mark Hamill. He shared the award with Disney animator Glen Keane.
